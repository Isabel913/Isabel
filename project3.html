PIR Motion Sensor

How PIR Motion Sensor works

As the PIR sensor detects a rabbit, the result in the console turns to "1". When there is no rabbit, the result stays as "0".

Website Display

As the PIR Motion Sensor detects a moving rabit and result in the console turns to "1", there will be a moving rabit appear on the website display, notifying me.
